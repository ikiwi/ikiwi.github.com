Christmas is my favorite season, and cranberry reminds me of christmas. I noticed that in SF there are alot more people with food allergy than other places that I've lived in. Here's a gluten free dessert for the holidays.

    In a double boiler, melt:
  • 250g unsalted butter
  • 160g valrhona white chocolate
    Once cooled, add in:
  • 150g egg yolk
    Stir in the following dry ingredients:
  • 250g chopped cranberry
  • zest of 2 oranges
    To turn these into a cake, whip meringue into stiff peak form:
  • 260g egg white
  • 210g sugar
    Instruction:
  • Fold the meringue into the mixture 1/3 at a time. Spread them on a sheet pan, and bake at 325F for 8 minutes.
  • To assemble, shape the flourless cake into a triangle form, sprinkle with mini pineapple chunks, side with vanilla ice cream, and top with lime zest.
